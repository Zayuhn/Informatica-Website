Fortnite

Epic Games, July 21, 2017

Trailer link

Fortnite is an online video game, created and released by epic games. There are three different game modes: Battle Royale, Save the World and Creative. The most popular game mode is Battle Royale, with six to twelve million players playing every day. The main focus of the game is to be the last player standing while fighting off 99 other players using guns and buildable fortifications.

Minecraft

Mojang Studios, November 18, 2011

Trailer link

Minecraft is an open-world video game made of cubes. You can play singleplayer survival, here your main goal is to survive as long as possible, and creative, where your main goal is to just build things that look good, but you can also play online minigames with your friends.
